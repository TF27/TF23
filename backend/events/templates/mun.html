Frequently Asked Questions

What is Model United Nations?


Model United Nations (also Model UN or MUN) is an academic simulation of the United Nations that aims to educate and encourage participants to discuss about major issues concerning the world, topics in international relations, diplomacy and the United Nations agenda.

What are the dates of Techfest World MUN?


Techfest World MUN, IIT Bombay is a two day conference and will be held during Techfest 2022-23 from the 17th to the 18th of December, 2022.

What awards will be provided at TWMUN 2022?


The awards will be presented at the discretion of the chairs of each committee and will include one Best Delegate and one Honourable Mentions/High Commendations. A Best Delegation award will also be provided.

Who can register for TWMUN, IIT Bombay?


All students with a valid ID card of their high school or college can register for TWMUN, IIT Bombay. However, exceptional cases are sometimes made for recent graduates.

When will the study guides be released?


The study guides will be released approximately one month before the conference.

What is the venue?


Techfest World Model United Nations will be held on the campus of IIT Bombay.

I am a first time MUNner. What should I expect?


An intellectual challenge! Follow basic steps, which include reading your study guides, being well prepared on your agendas, and being ready to speak during the conference. Take multiple opportunities and participate during the moderated and unmoderated caucus. Focus on managing your country’s interests and speaking clearly.

What is the dress code for Techfest World MUN 2022?


The Dress Code for Techfest World MUN 2022 is western formals/Indian formals.

About TWMUN



Techfest World Model United Nations is finally back offline, on the grounds of IIT Bombay. It acts as a benchmark platform for advanced debate on the social, political and economic facets of the world at large. Through its forum of excellence, Techfest World Model United Nations has created an unparalleled legacy of robust debate involving members of the youth from across the world. This year, Techfest World Model United Nations promises to be bigger, bolder and better.



About Techfest


Techfest is the Annual Science and Technology Festival of IIT Bombay, initiated in 1998 with a mission to promote technology, scientific thinking, and innovation. Now in its 26th Edition, Techfest has grown to be Asia’s Largest Science & Technology Festival with an astounding annual footfall of 1.75+ lakh, and an extensive network of over 3000 colleges, including more than 500 international universities. With a plethora of workshops, competitions, lectures, conferences, exhibitions, performances, concerts and various other events, Techfest is not just a college festival, it is an experience that you will cherish forever!



Welcome Letter


Despite the fact that time marches on, flaunting all of its unpredictable glory, Model United Nations Conferences have ensured our survival by connecting it to our capacity for change and providing us with a forum in which to voice our concerns without hindrance. We may have first become interested in Model United Nations competitions by the allure of sitting in a conference room where everyone is dressed impeccably, each person representing a country while discussing a global issue and attempting to find a solution to a problem that not even the world's best diplomats can solve, but our years as members of the Indian MUN Society have left a mark far more permanent than any award or certificate. MUNs have become a location for attacking, defending, and most importantly, compromising where through diplomacy and courtesy everyone strives to make "ladies and gentlemen" a reality. The members of the Indian MUN society represent the apex of a community of lifelong learners who seek knowledge not only from the voluminous electronic bookshelves of the world's leading universities but also from one another. If you're looking for a place with a unique culture of growth, go no further than the Techfest World Model United Nations, where we've adopted an inclusive society. Many late nights have been spent over the past few months by our committee's Secretariat and its unrivalled Executive board to ensure that you will leave Techfest with far more information and connections than you had before you arrived.


Tannusriya Tripathi

The General Assembly allocates to the Third Committee, agenda items relating to a range of social, humanitarian affairs and human rights issues that affect people all over the world.

The Committee also addresses important social development questions such as issues related to youth, family, ageing, persons with disabilities, crime prevention, criminal justice, and international drug control. The United Nations Security Council

The UNSC charter seeks to promote harmonious relationships among the member nations by maintaining international peace and security. The Security Council takes the lead in determining the existence of a threat to the peace or act of aggression. It calls upon the parties to a dispute to settle it by peaceful means and recommends methods of adjustment or terms of settlement.

The North Atlantic Treaty Organization

NATO is an active and leading contributor to peace and security on the international stage. It promotes democratic values and is committed to the peaceful resolution of disputes. However, if diplomatic efforts fail, it has the military capacity needed to undertake crisis-management operations, alone or in cooperation with other countries and international organisations. As the investigation into a deadly explosion in Polish territory continues, Poland’s government has reserved the right to invoke Article 4 of NATO’s treaty, a provision that enables members to start a formal discussion within the alliance about threats to their security.

United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees

UNHCR is a United Nations agency mandated to aid and protect refugees, forcibly displaced communities, persons facing discrimination on the basis of gender or sexual orientation, and stateless people, and to aid in their voluntary repatriation, local integration, or resettlement in a third country. UNHCR as a committee serves as a platform to have a solution-focused dialogue for improving the pre-existing system and providing LGBTQIA+ persons an opportunity to live freely and seek asylum without invasive questions into their traumatic experiences.

United Nations Commission on the Status of Women

There have been various successful initiatives to enhance women's rights, but it is equally essential that women's rights be protected on a worldwide scale. Despite the fact that women's rights have advanced to the point that they have nearly equal employment prospects and a greater social status in the majority of regions, women still maintain inferior positions to males in the workplace and in society for a variety of reasons. This is noted to be most prominent in Middle Eastern nations engaged in the difficult process of enacting social and political transformation, where the unequal status of women stands out as an especially formidable obstacle. Being devoted to the protection of women's human rights, the Commission on the Status of Women (CSW) provides a forum for representatives of United Nations institutions and civil society organisations to participate in issue-based advocacy dialogues on specific issues relevant to women's rights.

Agenda

Reforms for protection of women's rights and constitutional freedoms with special emphasis on middle eastern countries.

UN Women

A committee that became operational in 2011 thus becoming a landmark moment in streamlining the world’s focus on the plight of women, their empowerment and working towards the equality of both genders has placed the 2030 Sustainable Development Goals as one of the centres of their main thematic work. In the wise words of the first Executive Director of UNW, Ms. Michelle Bachelet, “Justice means more than punishing the perpetrators; it means redress and reparations that enable women to recover.”
Dedicated to gender equality and empowerment of women, UN Women serves as the global champion on women’s rights. One can expect various socio-political topics to be put up for debate in this committee in furtherance of the common aim to develop and uphold standards and create an environment in which every women and girl can exercise her human rights and live up to her full potential.
